Beyond Rules-Based Automation

Traditional automation handles simple, structured tasks. AI automation handles the messy, judgement-heavy work that previously required a human — reading documents, classifying requests, generating reports, or routing decisions based on nuanced criteria.

How It Works

1
Trigger
An event fires — a new form submission, email, Airtable record update, or scheduled run.
2
AI Reasoning
Data is passed to an LLM with a structured prompt — classify, summarise, extract, score, or generate.
3
Action
The AI output updates records, sends emails, generates documents, or routes to a human for review.
